The Camera

The camera can be in 4 positions: each corner

Controls

Camera

Scroll wheel: move camera up/down

Left / Right arrow: move camera clockwise/counterclockwise

Up/ Down arrow: change camera offset

Piece controls

i/k/j/l: move piece up/down/left/right

w/x: rotate x-y axis

a/d: rotate x-z axis

e/z: rotate y-z axis

think of rotations like a hexagon of keys around the s key

s: drop piece
